NARC 2020 Dates Announced

Each year the traditional end of the hurricane season is November 1st and Election Day is always 1st Tuesday in November. And we been offshore every election year
since 2000. Sometimes on our way to Bermuda, sometimes right after we depart

In 2020, waiting until after Election Day would mean moving the NARC departure date from New departure date from Newprot too late. So this year we will depart on Tuesday Oct 27th 2020. This date
still allows for a weather delay and time to reach Bermuda by election day results on Tuesday night, November 3rd. The fleet will depart Bermuda no earlier than Wednesday
November 4th and arrive in St. Maarten 5 or 6 days later. So now you have the dates for the start of the NARC Rally and everyone can schedule accordingly.

Your first offshore sailing experience

Swan Program: A First Experience with Offshore Sailing

If you've ever considering deepwater sailing but were not sure how to jump start your sailing career, our Program is for you.

Our Swan Program was specifically designed to introduce people to the world of offshore sailing by providing hands-on crew experience under the watchful eye of an
professional skipper on a proven boat. During your tenure onboard, you'll learn to keep watch, make log entries, navigate, use the radio, steer the boat and perform tasks that
are essentials to safe offshore sailing. The Swan Program offers a fixed for everything you'll need aboard--you just pay for your travel to and from the boat. The Swan
Program sails south from New England to Bermuda and the Carribean in the fall, and north from Bermuda and the Carribean to New England in the Spring. Summer trips are
also available.

After your first Swan Program experience, you'll have the skills to sail on other boats. Please view the video below to learn more.
